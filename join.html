What it means to Bee a Hero


When you join our project, we use 50% of our proceeds to sponsor beekeeping projects in low-income areas


This way, you will passively be helping out people all over the world with keeping our Bee population healthy and increasing the food supply where the Bees live.

You can use this form to either request more information about us and what we do, or to apply for a membership. After applying we will get in touch with you within 2-3 Business Days in order to get detailed insight in to your ambition and help map your needs in order for us to help you getting started as fast and simple as possible.

The Bee a Hero Project was founded in November 2020.
