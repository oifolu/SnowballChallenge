Bee's actual life purpose is to help pollination.


They live for helping spread the growth of plants all over the world
- why not give them a hand.

Here at the "Bee a Hero" project we aim to enable as many people as possible to start little beekeeping projects on their own.
Our goal is to help anyone who are willing to keep a struggling bee population thriving. A much overlooked fact is how important how bees contribution to plant pollination is crucial to our way of living.

Facts about pollination:
It is estimated that bees pollinate crops worth approximately 15 billion dollars each year, and that is only in the United States!

Our work

The Bee a Hero project is dedicated to assist anyone interested in getting started with beekeeping or maintaining a healty bee population where they live.
We provide the equipment and learning resources needed to get started, whether you want to explore this as a hobby or you plan to start a large scale beekeeping project - we will be able to assist you every step of the way.

The Bee a Hero Project was founded in November 2020.
